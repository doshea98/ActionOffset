Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026

Who we are

This website is operated by Action Offset ("we", "us"). For any privacy query, contact david@actionoffset.com.

What we collect

  • Information you send us: if you email us (e.g. to request a feasibility check), we receive your name, email address and the content of your message.
  • Business contact data: in the course of business development we may hold publicly available business contact details of organisations we believe may be interested in our services.

This is a static site and does not set non-essential cookies or run third-party trackers by default.

Why we use it (lawful basis)

We process the above to respond to enquiries and to carry out relevant, proportionate business-to-business outreach about our services, relying on our legitimate interests under the GDPR and Irish ePrivacy regulations. You can object or opt out at any time.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, correct, erase, or object to our processing of your personal data, and to lodge a complaint with the Irish Data Protection Commission (dataprotection.ie). To exercise any right, or to opt out of outreach, email david@actionoffset.com and we will action it promptly.

Retention

We keep enquiry and contact data only as long as necessary for the purpose above, and maintain a suppression list of anyone who has opted out so we do not contact them again.
